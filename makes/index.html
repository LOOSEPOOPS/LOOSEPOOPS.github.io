SSOO, Flagstaff, AZ

LOOSE POOPS is participating in Something Said Only Once on 2015.05.23 :: 5:00 p.m. at Firecreek Coffee Company in Flagstaff, AZ.

YOU ARE INVITED.

SSOO is a mini-festival taking place in Flagstaff during May 2015 that explores the meaning of the ἅπαξ λεγόμενον in the Internet age. LOOSE POOPS is exploring this space through a collaboratively-authored, semianonymized collection of ekphrastic collage. At the event, LOOSE POOPS will have craft supplies, photographs, and words that will, with the audience's assistance, be made into art. The audience is encouraged to participate in the event while watching the other performers and to assist in displaying the resulting artwork in hearts, on walls, and under rivers.
